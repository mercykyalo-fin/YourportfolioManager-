Quantitative Edge

My investment philosophy is rooted in quantitative analysis and robust risk management. I leverage statistical metrics (e.g., VaR, CVaR, Sharpe Ratio, Z-Score, T-Statistic, P-Value) to assess potential investments, manage tail risk, and ensure the statistical significance of observed performance. This data-driven approach minimizes emotional biases and promotes consistent, objective decision-making.
